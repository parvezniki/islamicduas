Duas for Laylatul Qadr

The Night of Decree — Better than a Thousand Months

✨ Make these Duas before Iftaar & throughout the blessed night — especially on odd nights: 21, 23, 25, 27, 29
Most Recommended Dua
★ Taught by the Prophet ﷺ to Aisha (RA)
اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّكَ عَفُوٌّ تُحِبُّ الْعَفْوَ فَاعْفُ عَنِّي
Allahumma innaka 'afuwwun, tuhibbul 'afwa, fa'fu 'anni
O Allah, You are the Pardoner, You love to pardon — so pardon me. (Repeat as much as possible this night)

Hamaare khataon ko darguzar farma.
O Allah, overlook and pardon our faults and errors.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Hame Lailatul Qadr ata farma.
O Allah, grant us the blessings of Laylatul Qadr.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Hame kaamil imaan naseeb farma aur poori hidayat farma.
O Allah, bless us with complete faith and perfect guidance.
Aye Allah ﷻ

O Allah, keep the blessed Kalima ever upon our tongues.

O Allah, fill us with the mercies, blessings and divine lights of the entire month of Ramzan.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Hamare dilon ko ikhlaas ke saath deen ki taraf pher de.
O Allah, turn our hearts sincerely towards Your deen.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Apni khaas rahmat naazil farma aur apne qahr wo azaab se bacha.
O Allah, shower Your special mercy upon us and protect us from Your wrath and punishment.

By the Beautiful Names of Allah
Ya Rahmaan, Ya Raheem, Ya Malik, Ya Quddus, Ya Salaam, Ya Ghaffaar, Ya Ghafoor, Ya Kareem — hamaare tamaam gunaahon ko maaf farma.
O Most Merciful, O Compassionate, O Sovereign, O Most Holy, O Source of Peace, O Oft-Forgiving, O Pardoner, O Most Generous — forgive all of our sins.
Seeking Forgiveness — Known & Unknown
Aye Allah ﷻ — hume gunaahon se nafrat de de. Ya Allah ﷻ — jaane anjaane mein humse jo galtiyan hui hain unhe maaf farma. Ya Allah ﷻ — hum jo galtiyon ki tauba karna bhool gaye, aap un galtiyon ko bhi maaf farma.
O Allah, make us hate our sins. O Allah, forgive us the mistakes we made knowingly and unknowingly. O Allah, forgive even those sins for which we forgot to repent.
For Our Departed Loved Ones
★ From the Sunnah — Dua for the Deceased
اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لَهُ وَارْحَمْهُ وَعَافِهِ وَاعْفُ عَنْهُ
Allahumma-ghfir lahu warhamhu wa 'afihi wa'fu 'anhu
O Allah, forgive him/her, have mercy on him/her, grant him/her well-being and pardon him/her. (From Sahih Muslim — recited by the Prophet ﷺ in funeral prayer)
★ From the Sunnah — Funeral Prayer Dua
اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِحَيِّنَا وَمَيِّتِنَا وَشَاهِدِنَا وَغَائِبِنَا وَصَغِيرِنَا وَكَبِيرِنَا وَذَكَرِنَا وَأُنْثَانَا
Allahumma-ghfir lihayyina wa mayyitina wa shaahidina wa ghaa'ibina wa sagheerina wa kabeerina wa dhakarena wa unthana
O Allah, forgive our living and our dead, those present and those absent, our young and our old, our males and our females. (Abu Dawood — recited by the Prophet ﷺ)
```
Aye Allah ﷻ
Tamaam marhumeen ko Jannat-ul-Firdaus ata farma, dozakh ke azaab se, qabar ke azaab se aur jahannam ki aag se bacha.
O Allah, grant all the deceased Jannat-ul-Firdaus and protect them from the punishment of hellfire, the grave and the fire of Jahannam.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Hamare marhumeen ke gunaahon ko maaf farma, un ki khataon se darguzar farma aur unhe apni rehmat ki chadar mein dhamp le.
O Allah, forgive the sins of our departed, overlook their faults and wrap them in Your blanket of mercy.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Hamare marhumeen ki qabrein noor se bhar de, unhe qabar ke tanhaapan mein uns aur sukoon ata farma.
O Allah, fill the graves of our departed with light, and grant them comfort and peace in the loneliness of the grave.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Hamare marhumeen ki qabrein unke liye jannat ka baghicha bana de, aur unhe qabar ke azaab se mahfooz rakh.
O Allah, make their graves a garden from the gardens of Paradise, and protect them from the punishment of the grave.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Hamare walidain — jab bhi unhe yaad karte hain — unhe apni rehmat se nawaaz, unke darjaat buland farma aur unhe Huzoor ﷺ ki rafaqat mein rakh.
O Allah, whenever we remember our parents, shower them with Your mercy, elevate their ranks and keep them in the company of the Prophet ﷺ.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Hamare marhumeen ke naam par jo bhi sadqa-e-jaariya, ilm aur nek aulaad ka silsila jaari hai, uska sawaab unhe pohanchata reh.
O Allah, let all ongoing charity, knowledge and righteous deeds done in their name continue to reach them as reward.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Hamare marhumeen ko Munkir wo Nakeer ke sawalaat se aasaani ata farma aur unhe seedha jawaab dene ki taufeeq de.
O Allah, ease the questioning of Munkir and Nakeer for our departed and grant them the right answers.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Hamare marhumeen ke liye Hashr ke din meezan mein nekiyan bhaari kar de aur unhe bina hisaab jannat mein daakhil farma.
O Allah, make their good deeds heavy on the Scale on the Day of Judgement and admit them into Paradise without reckoning.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Un tamaam logon ko bhi maaf farma jo is duniya se chale gaye aur jinka koi poochhne wala nahi — un yateemon, miskeenonke aur lawarisoon ko bhi jannat ata farma.
O Allah, also forgive those who left this world with no one to pray for them — the orphans, the poor and the forgotten — and grant them Paradise too.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Tamaam Ummat-e-Mohammadia ke marhumeen ko — jinhe hum jaante hain aur jinhe nahi jaante — apni rehmat aur maghfirat se nawaz.
O Allah, bless all departed Muslims of the Ummah — those we know and those we do not — with Your mercy and forgiveness.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Hamare marhumeen ko Qayamat ke din Huzoor ﷺ ke saath uthaa, unke haathon se Jaam-e-Kausar pilaa aur unhe Jannat mein hamare saath jama farma.
O Allah, resurrect our departed alongside the Prophet ﷺ, let them drink from Kausar from his blessed hands, and reunite us with them in Jannah.

📖 Quranic Dua for Departed Parents (Surah Al-Isra: 24)
رَّبِّ ارْحَمْهُمَا كَمَا رَبَّيَانِي صَغِيرًا
Rabbi-rhamhuma kama rabbayyaani sagheeraa
My Lord, have mercy upon them both as they raised me when I was small. — Recite this for your parents, whether living or departed.
📖 Dua of Ibrahim ﷺ for Believers (Surah Ibrahim: 41)
رَبَّنَا اغْفِرْ لِي وَلِوَالِدَيَّ وَلِلْمُؤْمِنِينَ يَوْمَ يَقُومُ الْحِسَابُ
Rabbana-ghfir li wa liwalidayya wa lil mu'mineena yawma yaqoomul hisaab
Our Lord, forgive me and my parents and the believers on the Day when the account is established.

Duas for Khair — All That is Good
★ From the Sunnah — Comprehensive Dua for Khair
اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ الْخَيْرَ كُلَّهُ عَاجِلَهُ وَآجِلَهُ مَا عَلِمْتُ مِنْهُ وَمَا لَمْ أَعْلَمْ
Allahumma inni as'alukal khayra kullahu — 'aajilahu wa aajilahu, ma 'alimtu minhu wa ma lam a'lam
O Allah, I ask You for all good — that which is immediate and that which is delayed, that which I know of and that which I do not know. (Sahih Ibn Hibban)
★ From the Sunnah — Seek Khair, Flee from Sharr
وَأَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الشَّرِّ كُلِّهِ عَاجِلِهِ وَآجِلِهِ مَا عَلِمْتُ مِنْهُ وَمَا لَمْ أَعْلَمْ
Wa a'oodhu bika minash-sharri kullihi — 'aajilihi wa aajilihi, ma 'alimtu minhu wa ma lam a'lam
And I seek Your refuge from all evil — that which is immediate and that which is delayed, that which I know of and that which I do not know. (Sahih Ibn Hibban — recite as continuation of the above)
```
Aye Allah ﷻ
Hamare ghar mein, hamare rishtedaaron mein, hamare maal mein aur hamare waqt mein khair aur barkat ata farma.
O Allah, place khair and barakah in our homes, our relationships, our wealth and our time.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Hamare andar ki khairiyat zahir kar de aur jo burai chhupi hai use khatam farma.
O Allah, bring out the goodness within us and eliminate the hidden evil inside us.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Hume khair ka daai bana de — aise ke hum har jagah khair failaayein.
O Allah, make us spreaders of goodness wherever we go.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Hamare muamalaat mein khair rakh — hamare aane mein aur jaane mein, hamare uthne mein aur baithne mein.
O Allah, place khair in all our affairs — in our coming and going, in our rising and sitting.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Jo rizq Tu ne hamare liye likha hai use khair ke saath de, aur jis mein khair nahi us se door rakh.
O Allah, give us the sustenance You have decreed for us with goodness, and keep us away from that in which there is no good.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Hamare bachon ki zindagi mein khair ka rasta khol de aur unhe burai ke raston se bachaa le.
O Allah, open the paths of goodness in our children's lives and shield them from the roads of evil.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Hamare rishton mein khair rakh — mehram ho ya ghair mehram, dost ho ya parosee — sab ke saath hamaari guftugu aur muamalat khair par ho.
O Allah, place goodness in all our relationships — with family, friends and neighbours — let our words and dealings always be rooted in khair.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Hamare safaroon mein khair rakh — duniyawi safar ho ya aakhirat ka safar — dono mein khair aur salamati naseeb farma.
O Allah, place goodness in all our journeys — both worldly travels and the final journey to the hereafter — and grant us safety in both.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Jo baat hum bolte hain, jo kaam hum karte hain, jo sochte hain — un sab mein khair rakh aur unhe apni raza ke mutaabiq bana de.
O Allah, place goodness in everything we say, do and think — and align it all with Your pleasure.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Jo khair Tu ne dusron ko diya hai us mein haasad se bacha, aur hamare hisse mein bhi waise hi khair aur barkat likhi de.
O Allah, protect us from jealousy over the goodness You have given to others, and write for us our own share of khair and blessing.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Is raat mein — Lailatul Qadr ki is mubarak raat mein — hamare liye wo khair likh de jo hamare anumaan se bhi pare ho.
O Allah, on this blessed night of Laylatul Qadr, decree for us a goodness beyond what we can even imagine.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Hamare dushmanon ke shar se, zamane ke fitno se aur nafs ki burai se hume mahfooz rakh aur in sab ke badle mein khair ata farma.
O Allah, protect us from the harm of our enemies, the trials of the age and the evil of our own selves — and replace all of it with goodness.
Aye Allah ﷻ
Hamare khatme ko khair par rakh — akhri saans mein kalma-e-tayyaba aur tere saath khushnudi naseeb farma.
O Allah, seal our endings with goodness — grant us the Kalima on our last breath and let us meet You in a state of Your pleasure.

📖 Dua of Musa ﷺ for Khair (Surah Al-Qasas: 24)
رَبِّ إِنِّي لِمَا أَنزَلْتَ إِلَيَّ مِنْ خَيْرٍ فَقِيرٌ
Rabbi inni lima anzalta ilayya min khayrin faqeer
My Lord, I am in absolute need of whatever good You send down to me. — The dua of Musa ﷺ when he was alone, tired and in need. Recite this whenever you feel helpless and are seeking Allah's goodness.
📖 Dua for Khair in All Affairs (Surah Al-Kahf: 10)
رَبَّنَا آتِنَا مِن لَّدُنكَ رَحْمَةً وَهَيِّئْ لَنَا مِنْ أَمْرِنَا رَشَدًا
Rabbana aatina min ladunka rahmatan wa hayyi' lana min amrina rashada
Our Lord, grant us mercy from Yourself and prepare for us right guidance in our affair. — Dua of the People of the Cave, seeking khair and right direction directly from Allah.
📖 Dua for Khair in Deen, Dunya & Aakhirah (Sahih Muslim)
اللَّهُمَّ أَصْلِحْ لِي دِينِي الَّذِي هُوَ عِصْمَةُ أَمْرِي وَأَصْلِحْ لِي دُنْيَايَ الَّتِي فِيهَا مَعَاشِي وَأَصْلِحْ لِي آخِرَتِي الَّتِي فِيهَا مَعَادِي
Allahumma aslih li deeniyalladhi huwa 'ismatu amri, wa aslih li dunyayallatii fiha ma'aashi, wa aslih li aakhiratiyllatii fiha ma'aadi
O Allah, set right my deen which is the safeguard of my affairs; set right my worldly life in which is my livelihood; and set right my hereafter to which is my return. (Sahih Muslim)
For the Entire Ummah
Aye Allah ﷻ — tamaam Ummat-e-Muslima ki jaayez duaen qubool farma. Aye Allah ﷻ — hamaari duaon ko apne fazl-o-karam se, apne rahm-o-karam se qubool farma.
O Allah, accept the lawful duas of the entire Muslim Ummah. O Allah, accept our duas through Your grace, favour and limitless mercy.
Rabbana Dua with Full Extensions
رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ
Rabbana aatina fid-dunya hasanatan wa fil-aakhirati hasanatan waqina 'azaaban naar
Our Lord, give us good in this world and good in the Hereafter, and protect us from the punishment of the Fire.
